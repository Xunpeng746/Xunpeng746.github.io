Overview
Angle-Calibration Moment method (ACMo) is a novel stochastic optimization method enjoying the benefits of second moments with only first moment updates. Different from previous adaptive gradient methods (AGMs), ACMo has robust tier-1 performances in both image classification and neural machine translation tasks without over-designed iteration paradigms. This is achieved by: (a) understanding the geometric properties of second moments in Adam-type optimizers and (b) replacing the projection penalty in the AdGrad's proximal subproblem to a inner-product regularization.
